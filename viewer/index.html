Welcome to Panaderia Libre, an open-source bakery dedicated to empowering bread enthusiasts, bakers, and hobbyists to share, learn, and collaborate. Here, we provide meticulously crafted bread formulas, ready-to-make recipes, and specialty mixtures designed to inspire creativity and community. By embracing open-source principles, we aim to make baking knowledge accessible to all, encouraging innovation and the sharing of ideas. You won’t have to scroll through a soul-searching odyssey about how we discovered the sublime cosmic gaze of sourdough in the basement of a Tibetan monastery, where a wise monk handed us an ancient starter and told us it carried the memories of a thousand generations of bread. Just straightforward bread formulas, tools, and information to help you bake with confidence and creativity.
